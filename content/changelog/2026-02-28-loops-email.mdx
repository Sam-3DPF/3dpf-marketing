We migrated our email platform to [Loops.so](https://loops.so), which means better deliverability and smarter onboarding sequences for new trial users.

**What changed for trial users:** The day 3, 6, and 7 nudge emails now trigger based on actual in-app activity, not just signup date. If you've already connected Etsy and started tracking orders, you'll get emails that match where you are — not generic "have you tried X?" messages.

**Better deliverability.** Loops uses dedicated sending infrastructure, so transactional emails (order confirmations, sync alerts) land in inboxes more reliably. If you were missing emails before, that should be resolved.

No action needed on your end. Everything carries over automatically.
