title: "Etsy order sync"
date: "2026-03-03"
category: "Release"
---

Etsy orders now sync automatically into your 3D PrintForce dashboard. No more manually copying orders from Etsy into a spreadsheet.

**How it works:** Once you connect your Etsy shop, orders import on a 4-hour cycle. Every order lands in your dashboard with the buyer details, SKU, quantity, and fulfillment deadline already filled in.

**Status tracking built in.** Each order moves through your standard workflow: New → Printing → Printed → Shipped. Your print queue stays organized and up to date.

**SKU matching.** If the Etsy SKU matches a model you've already set up in 3D PrintForce, your cost breakdown and profit margin calculate automatically. For new SKUs, you'll get a prompt to link the order to an existing model or create a new one.

Etsy sync is available on the Growth plan. Connect your shop from the Integrations tab in your dashboard settings.
